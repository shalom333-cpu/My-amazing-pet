Myths and Misconceptions about the Bulldog

There are many myths and misconceptions about this amazing dog that lead most people to view it diffrently and in extreme cases mistreat this dog and use them for the wrong reasons. Sadly, this has lead to most of this dogs being euthanised which is such a sad thing. Bulldogs are just like any other dog species that need to be taken care of, loved and protected.
